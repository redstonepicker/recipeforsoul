Korea beef soup with rice.

Cooking ingredients

  • Beef Brisket or Shin 300g.
  • Korean radish 200g.
  • Bean sprouts 200g.
  • Onion 1/2.
  • Green onion 1.
  • Oyster Mushroom 100g.
  • Korean green chilli pepper or green chilli pepper. 1
  • Korean red chilli peepper or red chilli peeper 1.
  • Korean red pepper power 3T.
  • Soy source for soup(국간장) 3.5T.
  • Minced garlic 2T.
  • Korean Salted shrimp 2T.
  • Sesame Oil 2T.
  • Black peeper 1/3t

Cooking process.

  1. Remove blood from the beef using a kitchen towel.
  2. Cut the radish into 3cm pieces, both horizontally and vertically.
  3. Cut vegetables.
    1. Slice half an onion.
    2. Slice one green onion into 5cm lengths.
    3. Slice one green/red chili pepper.
    4. Tear the oyster Mushrooms vertically.
    5. Rinse the bean sprouts in cold water.
  4. In a large pan, 2 tablespoons of sesame oil and beef stir-fry medium heat.
  5. Once the red color on the surface of the beef disappears, add the radish and continue to stir-fry.
  6. Add 3 tablespoons of red pepper flakes and stir-fry without letting it burn until the radish is well coated with the color.
  7. Add 1.5 liters of water, cover with a lid, and boil over high heat for 10 minutes.
  8. Add sliced onions, oyster Mushrooms, and 3.5 tablespoons of soy sauce, then continue boiling over high heat for an additional 3 minutes.
  9. Add chili peppers (optional), 2 tablespoons of minced garlic, and bean sprouts, and continue to boiling.
